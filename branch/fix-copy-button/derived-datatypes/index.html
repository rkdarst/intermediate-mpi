Derived datatypes¶

Questions

  • How can you reduce the number of messages sent and received?

  • How can you use your own derived datatypes as content of messages?

Objectives

  • Understand how MPI handles datatypes and the typemap concept.

  • Learn to send and receive composite messages with MPI_Pack and MPI_Unpack

  • Learn how to represent homogeneous collections as MPI messages. MPI_Type_contiguous, MPI_Type_vector, MPI_Type_indexed

  • Learn how to represent your own derived datatypes as MPI messages with MPI_Type_create_struct and MPI_Type_commit

  • MAYBE: Learn how to define combination operations on your datatypes: MPI_Op_create and MPI_Op_free

Datatype constructors in MPI¶

  • Contiguous types: MPI_Type_contiguous

  • Extent-strided types: MPI_Type_vector

  • Byte-strided types: MPI_Type_create_hvector

  • Extent-indexed types: MPI_Type_indexed

  • Byte-strided types: MPI_Type_create_hindexed

  • General types: MPI_Type_create_struct

  • Register and unregister your types: MPI_Type_commit, MPI_Type_free
